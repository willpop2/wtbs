How it works

Every interview transcript can be corrected by readers. Highlight the words you want to fix on any episode page and suggest an edit. We review each one by hand — if we accept it, it's applied to the transcript, logged in that episode's change log, and credited to your handle here. Because every edit is reviewed before it counts, the board can't be gamed by volume, and a ✓ marks handles we've confirmed. Ranking is by episodes improved, then total edits. The show's own hosts aren't listed — this board is for the readers.
