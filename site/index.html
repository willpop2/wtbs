About the show

Waiting To Be Signed is a podcast hosted by Will and Trinity about generative and on-chain art. Alongside the weekly news show, they sit down with the artists, collectors, and platform builders behind fx(hash), Art Blocks, and Verse. This archive collects the interviews with full, readable transcripts — 215 episodes deep, and we're not done yet. Spot an error on any transcript and you can suggest a fix.
