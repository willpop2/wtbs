Episode: There Is No Offline: Interview with Maya Man
Guest(s): Maya Man

WTBS welcomes Maya Man to the show for a fantastic interview episode. Maya has an amazing Verse Solo's exhibition releasing this week, "I'm Feeling Lucky" that explores the relationship between astrology, numerology, and The Internet. Please go check it out! In this episode we discuss: A brief into of how Maya got into making art with code & releasing projects as NFTs Getting exposed to astrology in LA and the online structures of identity The logistics of building "I'm Feeling Lucky" and how it relates to "Fake It 'Till You Make It" The personal aspect of "Read It And Weep" Cyborgs and feminism Growing up online, the fear + shame of posting, and the performance of 'self' on the internet How astrology and generative art overlap Finding comfort in simulation theory and determinism Humor in art and the internet Rapid Fire Questions! Big thanks to Maya for taking the time to record with us!
