Episode: Buying Through the Bear Market: Interview with KRPDM DAO
Guest(s): KRPDM DAO

Trinity and Will interview Ed, one of the founding members of the KRPDM (Carpe Diem). KRPDM is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization [DAO] that collects NFTs from all corners of the Tezos eco-system. From the outset they've had a clear directive to collect gen-art, and see fx(hash) as cultural epicenter for long form gen-art on Tezos. In this episode we discuss the logistics of collecting as an organization, optimism about the Tezos blockchain, the DAO's bullishness despite the impending bear market, and of course, THE ART.
