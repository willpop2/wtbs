Episode: Translating Hand to Code: Interview with William Mapan
Guest(s): William Mapan

WTBS is pleased to bring you another exciting interview episode. We were fortunate to record with William a few weeks before his Verse Solos release, Intimacy . Unfortunately Trinity could not make the recording session for this one :( In this episode we discuss: William's background in art and coding & how he found the blockchain The early days of Tezos & fx(hash) and how Dragons became one of the 'grail' projects of the platform. Anticyclone and the lessons learned from Dragons that made for his successful release on Art Blocks Curated. William's philosophy around trat distribution, rarity & the floor pieces of Distance , his most recent release with LACMA Sketchbook A & the process for writing an algorithm to mimic a physical medium How Sketchbook A will be released, going small, and making the leap to 'collector curated' How Through Your Eyes relates and contrasts with Sketchbook A Williams paintings, future plans for the Sketchbook series, and of course... rapid fire :) Thanks to William for taking the time to come on the show and speak to us for this interview!
