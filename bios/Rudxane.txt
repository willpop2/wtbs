Episode: Imperfect Perfectionism: Interview with Rudxane
Guest(s): Rudxane

WTBS is back with another interview episode, this time with fx(hash) legend Rudxane! We've been fans of Rudxane for as long as we have been collecting generative art. In this episode we talk about the humanity of imperfection in art, the virtues of releasing on other chains/platforms (including Bitcoin), long lost collaborations, what to expect in 2023, going "physical", the bear market shakeout + more! Big thanks to Rudxane for coming on the show. Follow Rudxane on Twitter @rudxane1 and check out his website for info on all his work.
