Episode: Computational Creativity: Interview with Erik Swahn
Guest(s): Erik Swahn

Join us as we welcome fx(hash) & overall genart legend Erik Swahn to his very first podcast appearance! We have been fans and collectors of Erik for 2+ years now and are thrilled to have him on the show to discuss his practice & upcoming release, Autoscope, on Verse In this episode we discuss: Erik's background in art & coding and how he discovered NFTs as a way to release his work Erik's earliest fx(hash) releases, Retrogrades and Disegnatori , and how they play into the overall themes of his work Farbteiler's origin as a synesthetic system for learning piano + palettes and color construction Punktwelt , Fields & Erik's current style as it has evolved from Farbteiler Embracing 'Collector Curated' as a mechanism for release & collectors hitting the mark in their selections Maintaining a consistent visual style without fatiguing collectors Autoscope on Verse & adding animation to create a sense of place to the work Releasing with Verse for Erik's last 3 projects and how he thinks about platforms and blockchains Rapid Fire!
