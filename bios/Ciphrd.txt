Episode: Unlocking A Generative Future: Interview with Ciphrd
Guest(s): Ciphrd

Welcome to our third interview episode with Ciphrd, the creator of fx(hash). In this episode we discuss the the recently announced funding that will be used to expand fx(hash) to the Ethereum blockchain along with other features slated for the fx(hash) 2.0 release. We also get Ciphrd's take on the current generative art NFT ecosystem, the recent influx of new platforms, and ideas for future-proofing fx(hash) for the next bear market. We hope you enjoy this one as much as we did. Big thanks to Ciphrd and the team for coordinating this episode with us.
