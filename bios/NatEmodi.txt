Episode: Building The Generative Bazaar: Interview with Nat Emodi, founder of Highlight.xyz
Guest(s): Nat Emodi, founder of Highlight.xyz

In this special interview episode we welcome Nat Emodi, founder and CEO of Highlight.xyz. We learned about highlight recently for their participation in the On-Chain Summer event hosted on the Base chain. Thanks to Nat for coming on and sharing his vision for Highlight and the future of generative art! In this episode we discuss: How Nat got into crypto & generative art and the story of the founding of Highlight Why another platform? What makes Highlight different and what are the benefits (and risks) of giving artists the tools to own their own contract Ethereum, ETH L2s, and the UX challenges of the current crypto experience. Highlight's economic model & betting on the potential of generative art Highlight's role in homepage curation & collaborating with artists Royalties!
