Episode: Legitimizing The Space: Interview with Jamie Gourlay
Guest(s): Jamie Gourlay

Jamie Gourlay is co-founder of verse.works , a curated digital art collecting platform that has featured many artists we know and love from fx(hash). In our longest interview yet, Will and Trinity dive into Jamie's background in the traditional art world, the challenges of NFTs as a brand, and a long discussion on the importance of markets and how to legitimize the space to make it more appealing to new collectors. This episode is especially timely considering the renewed debate about creator royalties and the non-enforcement policy just announced by OpenSea.
