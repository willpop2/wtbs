Guest: Jimmy Griffith, who goes by the handle "remnynt" (also styled Remnynt).
A systems / generative artist from North Carolina (b. 1985, Charlotte, NC). Holds a
BS in Computer Science from UNC Chapel Hill. Previously worked in the games industry,
where he co-developed the award-winning game "EverWing" (2016).

His generative art works include: "vibes" (2021) and "the reliquary" (2022).
Recent projects: "Architectonica" and "Proscenium" (released on Art Blocks, late 2024).
