Episode: Information Asymmetries: Interview with Kyloren
Guest(s): Kyloren

Another legendary collector joins us for over an hour of market and collecting talk. Kylo was one of the first to mint a Squiggle on Art Blocks and was one of the earliest big collectors on fx(hash) as well. We talk to him about his general thesis for collecting generative art NFTs, what is was like back in the "old days" of fx(hash), why he is starting his ALFA fund, and plenty of artist and project talk! Big thanks to Kylo for making the time and joining us from half way around the world to get this episode recorded.
