Episode: The Distance of a Bird: Interview with Iskra Velitchkova
Guest(s): Iskra Velitchkova

WTBS is thrilled to have Iskra Velitchkova in the virtual studio for a long-overdue interview! In this episode we discuss: Iskra's background in art, coding & data science and how she transitioned to creating art full time. Finding inspiration to start working through books & "The Pause" The connections of places to moments in Iskra's work & the transition to 'long form' generative art with Uninhabitable. Will we ever recapture that feeling of HEN & early fx(hash) community? Defining success and deciding between chains and platforms to release projects The 'generative religion' of the NFT collector base & how collectors evaluate and define what they want to buy The big question: What can we learn about ourselves through technology? The potential to incorporate data into future projects What might we see (or smell) from Iskra in the future? The personal choice & struggle around committing to art as a career A very brief Rapid Fire! Thanks to Iskra for coming on the show and sharing so much with us.
