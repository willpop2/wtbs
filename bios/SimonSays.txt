Episode: Helping Artists Achieve Their Goals: Interview with SimonSays
Guest(s): SimonSays

SimonSays is a generative art collector and appreciator that recently converted his love for Art into a career as an artist manager & agent. In this interview we dive into Simon's background in art and collecting, how he linked up with the artists he represents, his mission for enabling artists to achieve their goals, and a whole lot of market talk! Huge thanks to SimonSays for taking the time to come on the show and speak candidly about his business.
