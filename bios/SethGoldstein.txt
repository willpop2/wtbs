Episode: Peak Bright Moments: Interview with Seth Goldstein
Guest(s): Seth Goldstein

Will and Trinity interview Seth from Bright Moments just days before the start of their massive Tokyo event. In this episode we learn about Seth's background in art and technology, the early days of Bright Moments and the culture of Venice Beach, the importance of live events and physical friction in collecting art, the future of Bright Moments, the health of the market and a lot more. Huge thanks to Seth for taking the time to talk to us all the way from Tokyo, we are thrilled to share this episode with you.
