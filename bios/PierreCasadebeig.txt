Episode: Minimum Viable Mountain: Interview with Pierre Casadebeig
Guest(s): Pierre Casadebeig

Welcome to another interview episode! We are thrilled to be chatting with Pierre Casadebeig ahead of his long-awaited Tender collaboration: Ridge Regressions. In this episode we discuss: Who is Pierre & how did he get into making art?
