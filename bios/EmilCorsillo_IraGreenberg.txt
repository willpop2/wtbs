Episode: What Is Long-Form Generative A.I. Art?: Interview with Emil Corsillo & Ira Greenberg
Guest(s): Emil Corsillo & Ira Greenberg

Will and Trinity sit down with Emil and Ira (two of the five founders of emprops.ai , the long-form generative AI platform) to learn about the origins of Emergent Properties and answer the burning question: What is long-form generative A.I.??? Ira fills us in on how he made The Oracles , the smash-hit "genesis" project of the platform, and we get a peak at the process behind Emil's just-released project, Ghost in the Machine . We also chat them up about their own extensive histories in art, their work and collecting on fxhash, and music recommendations!
