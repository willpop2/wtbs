Episode: Physicals in a Digital Art World: Interview with Susannah Maybank, co-founder of Tonic.xyz
Guest(s): Susannah Maybank, co-founder of Tonic.xyz

Susannah Maybank is the co-founder of Tonic.xyz , a generative art platform that is built around the production of high quality prints for each of their releases. Susannah crossed over from the legacy art world and has a passion for educating collectors about generative art & NFTs. In this episode we discuss: Susannah's background and her career in the legacy art world The story behind the founding of Tonic and the early days of the company How Tonic works to cross over new collectors to the space & what the major challenges are in educating collectors around NFTs The challenges & process of coming up with novel print options for Tonic and what kind of engagement they are seeing on these offerings How Tonic identifies & pitches artists that they want to work with and what that process is like from start to release Will NFT art markets start to become more like traditional markets? The tension between market forces and algorithmic potential, and why it can be good to constrain the number of outputs for a release Excitement for a digitally native art movement & the need for more criticism and conversation around NFTs.
