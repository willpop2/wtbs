Episode: Perfection Isn't Interesting: Interview with Frank Manzano & Artie Handz
Guest(s): Frank Manzano & Artie Handz

WTBS is back with our first interview of 2024, a special episode with artist Frank Manzano & collector/curator Artie Handz! The Artie Gallery is bringing "ScheduledProgramming" to Verse on January 11, 2024.
