Episode: Feeling Infinity: Interview with LoVid (Tali Hinkis & Kyle Lapidus)
Guest(s): LoVid (Tali Hinkis & Kyle Lapidus)

NYC duo LoVid are in the studio and sharing a mic for a very special WTBS interview. Please check out their upcoming release on Tonic, Heartsleeves , which we discuss a bit in the episode.
