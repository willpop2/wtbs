Episode: Uncompromising Collaborations: Interview with Rich Poole & Rick Crane
Guest(s): Rich Poole & Rick Crane

Rich & Rick, two legendary fx(hash) collaborators, finally get the WTBS treatment! In this episode we discuss: Their backgrounds as artists and the origin of their collaborative practice The story behind ' Miniscapes ', their first collab project on fx(hash) Their collaborative process and how Rich brings the "generative-ness" to Rick's design & aesthetic + the challenges of adaptation. The story of Acequia and breaking through to a wider set of collectors outside of fx(hash) The process behind IsoMetro, their upcoming release, and how they came to release with 8NAP Will there be cars in IsoMetro & how it relates to Acequia How do Rich & Rick think about the market now, picking platforms and chains for release Rich "accidentally" donating his only Acequia to WTBS Rapid Fire! Thanks to Rich & Rick for coming on the show!
