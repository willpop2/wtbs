Episode: Building The Museum: Interview with LeMonde2D
Guest(s): LeMonde2D

WTBS is bringing you another special collector interview! We sit down with Didi aka Lemonde2d and walk through his whirlwind history collecting generative art on fxhash. In this episode we discuss building the ultimate GenArt Museum, why he goes so deep on some collections, and the importance of price (or lack thereof).
