Episode: Evolving Verse: Interview with Jamie Gourlay, Founder of Verse
Guest(s): Jamie Gourlay, Founder of Verse

We welcome Jamie for a return interview, a little over one year later since we last had him on! In this episode we discuss: Big lessons and takeaways for Jamie & Verse coming out of the 2023 bear market Consolidation of artists towards curated platforms & competing for releases Verse as a conduit for the gallery art world to access web3 and what exciting release they have coming soon as a test case Managing web3 communities as an artist and the need to be connected & authentic Verse becoming more web3 oriented with recent announcements The role of the market in onboarding new collectors & how new artists can break through without using open platforms Verse collecting & supporting artists that release with them Solana & the potential for Verse to add other chains moving forward Reacting to the recent announcement from Art Blocks & cross-platform collaborations The goal of a potential Verse community token Thanks to Jamie!
