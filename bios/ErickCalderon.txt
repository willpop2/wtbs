Episode: Magic Is Not Real: Interview with Erick Calderon (Round 2)
Guest(s): Erick Calderon (Round 2)

Erick is back for Round 2 to discuss more about the market, web3 as a whole, the changes to AB and more! In this episode we discuss: Catching up on Erick's experiences since our last interview & skepticism of brand's with a web 3 strategy The magic between an artist and their collector base & the metrics for success in the current ecosystem Base chain, the power & sustainability of 'free' drops, the success of Pudgy Penguins Onboarding collectors to generative art vs. web 3 as a whole Addressing the current market with the new Curated and raising the bar with future releases to bring excitement back to collecting Studio as a platform for artists to document all aspects of their work on-chain The problem of preserving digital art long-term What kind of generative goods could WTBS make as merchandise??
