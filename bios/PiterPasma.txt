Episode: Playing Code Golf: Interview with Piter Pasma
Guest(s): Piter Pasma

Special bonus interview with Piter Pasma! Piter has been making art with code for decades, but you may know him best for the work he has released on fxhash ( Hypergiraffe , Dot Produkt , Angry Noise , Gonzion ) and Art Blocks ( Skulptuur ). In this episode we learn about about Piter's history in code and code-based art, the origins of his long-form generative works, the beauty of code optimization, and the challenges of releasing art as NFTs.
