Episode: Tuning The Frequency: Interview with Chris McCully
Guest(s): Chris McCully

Chris McCully veteran fx(hash) creator, joins the WTBS crew in a special interview episode. Chris talks to us about his background in film and design and how it shaped his early success on the platform. We hear more about Bedlam, Chris's original "magnum opus", and go deep on some of his popular recent releases. Chris also gives us a HEN history lesson! Truly something for everyone in this one.
