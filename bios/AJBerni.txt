Episode: Hooked on Art: Interview with AJBerni
Guest(s): AJBerni

Will and Trinity sit down with AJBerni to learn the story of TENDER. Adam shares his background in art and design, how Tender collaborations work, the decision to sell the Tender Pass, and what we have to look forward to over the second half of the year.
