Episode: Structural Complexity Through Visual Clarity: Interview with Kjetil Golid
Guest(s): Kjetil Golid

The interview streak continues! This week we are excited to talk to Kjetil Golid ahead of his Monument release on Verse . In this episode we discuss: Kjetil's background in art and coding & how he first discovered the blockchain. The history behind Archetype and Paper Armada , how they came to Art Blocks and released so close together (plus how one collector traded a Fidenza for an Armada!) Creating systems and whether the art is the code or the output Kjetil's use of color and evolving palettes over time The story behind Monument, the influence of brutalist design in Kjetil's work, and returning to long form The change in royalties on Ethereum and how Kjetil thinks about chains and platforms for releasing projects\ Rapid fire! Thanks to Kjetil for coming on the show.
