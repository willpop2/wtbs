Episode: Living With Images: Interview with Zach Lieberman
Guest(s): Zach Lieberman

Another hot interview to end the August podcast hiatus. Will & Trinity had the excellent opportunity to sit down with Zach Lieberman just ahead of his Verse Solos exhibition, Studies in Color, Light and Geometry . We talk to Zach about using the blockchain to preserve art, what he looks for when curating work for release, the challenges of long form, the importance of prints and much more. We hope you enjoy this episode. Big thanks to Zach for taking the time to record with us! Follow Zach on Twitter @zachlieberman and instagram to check out all the amazing works in progress he shares.
